Who are we?

We are a group of girls located in Bay Area California trying to learn more about STEM. This is our first project in the program HSEP.

Purpose

We hope to match clubs and businesses together in a way that can benefit both. A business or club can fill out our questionnaire and we will process this information and find which club matches your wants and needs best. But, of course, our matches may not be perfect for you. So, we also are a resource to find clubs or businesses in your area all in one place.
