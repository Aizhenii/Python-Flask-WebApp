Boba Bots

Millbrae


https://www.bobabots253.org/

STEM, Robotics

FRC team founded in 1998 based in Mills High School, Millbrae, CA. Composed of four technical and soft skill departments including construction (builds the robot), electronics (wires the bot), programming (codes for the bot), and operations (publicity and strategy). Boba Bots attend several local California FRC competitions throughout the year and their purpose is to “build bots, build people, and build communiTEA.”

PROVIDES: Publicity on merchandise, robot, Instagram. Ethnically diverse (majority AAPI), almost 50% female/non-binary, almost 50% LGBTQ+

LOOKING FOR: Money, mentorship, outreach opportunities

CONTACT: mrteam@gmail.com

Girls Who Code

Millbrae


https://millsgwc.carrd.co/

STEM

Girls Who Code is an international organization dedicated to empowering women in STEM. Members in Mills High Schools Girls Who Code club learn the basics of creating personal websites with HTML, basic python coding, and Scratch block coding. The club attends multiple hackathons, STEM speaker events, and hosts community coding classes for Millbrae youth throughout the year on top of their personal curriculum and projects.

PROVIDES: Publicity on merchandise & Instagram. All female demographic.

LOOKING FOR: Money, mentorship, outreach opportunities

CONTACT: MillsHighGWC@gmail.com

Animated Film Development Club

Castro Valley


Arts

The premise of this club is to provide a creative space for any aspiring animators, artists, writers, voice actors, sound designers, and cinematographers to collaborate on a project, attempting to mimic actual professional animation development. The goal is to have a full animatic by the end of the year, complete with sound fx and voice overs.

PROVIDES: A promotion animation, wide variety of different skill sets including artists, writers, animators, voice actors, sound designers, musicians, video editors, and more.

LOOKING FOR: Opportunities to utilize skill sets, building portfolio, funding for equipment such as tablets and microphones

CONTACT: 45902@cv.k12.ca.us

Backroads Magazine

Martinez


Arts - Writing

The Backroads Magazine is an opportunity for newer writers, poets, and artists to share their creations with other with similar interests. The club also meets once a month to grab a cup of coffee and treats while some of the artists share their work.

PROVIDES: Publicity within their magazine, can organize and format entries to share with others.

LOOKING FOR: More entries, funding, and publicity.

CONTACT: backroadsmagazine@gmail.com
